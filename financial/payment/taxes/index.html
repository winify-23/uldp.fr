Tax Information

  • A 1098T form is available on-line through your myUH account effective January 29th of each year.
  • For questions, such as calculated dollar amounts on the 1098T form, please call 713-743-1010, option 6.
  • For information regarding Form 1098-T and how your Federal income tax return may be affected, please consult your tax preparer or visit the IRS website.
