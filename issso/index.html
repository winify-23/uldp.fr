WELCOME TO ISSSO

The International Student and Scholar Services Office (ISSSO) supports Student Affairs and the Dean of Students by addressing the unique needs of international students and exchange visitors. It ensures compliance with U.S. immigration laws and promotes internationalization through expert advising, services, and programs.

New International Student Orientation

January 15th, 2026

International Student Orientation is mandatory for all students holding non-immigrant visas and/or with pending permanent residency. The session will address unique content to cover restrictions and benefits to your visa status, the academic system and student services offered at the University of Houston, as well as American friendship patterns, language, and cultural adjustment.
