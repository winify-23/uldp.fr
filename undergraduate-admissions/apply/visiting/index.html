Visiting Students

A visiting student (sometimes referred to as a transient student) can take undergraduate courses at the University of Houston but is not seeking a degree. These students are getting a jumping start on their college coursework, applying credit toward a degree at another institution or may have already completed a bachelor's degree and are taking pre-requisite courses as a post-baccalaureate student.

Admission as a visiting student is granted to:

  • undergraduate students who are in good standing at their current or most recently attended college or university, and have a cumulative GPA of 2.0, or
  • high school graduates who have not earned college credit post high school graduation, and have a minimum unweighted cumulative GPA of a 3.5
  • visiting students that satisfy the English language requirement for UH

Visiting students are not eligible for financial aid.

Steps to Apply

  1. Complete the Transient Application at ApplyTexas.
  2. Pay the non-refundable $75 application fee ($90 for international students) with a credit card or debit card.
  3. Meet the English language requirement for UH.
  4. Send your transcript to us electronically. For more information about transcript submission guidelines, please visit the Submitting Official High School and College Transcripts Guidelines webpage.

We encourage you to look up the courses you plan to take in the UH Undergraduate Catalog. You must meet all course prerequisites to enroll. If courses you plan to take have prerequisites that you have taken at another college or university,  submit your official transcript(s) as soon as possible. Allow three to four weeks to process your transcript and post approved transfer credit. Submitting transcripts later than this timeline may not allow enrollment for that semester. 

See the Visiting Student Next Steps Page to understand your next steps after admission.

**Supporting information includes application fee and official transcript(s) from all attended institution(s).

Visiting to Degree Seeking

Once admitted as a visiting student, no application is necessary for enrollment in future term sessions. Enrollment as a degree-seeking student requires a visiting student to complete the Degree Seeking Status Change Request Form, meet UH transfer admission requirements (and their intended major) and submit all transcripts from previous colleges and universities. Admitted students will be required to go through New Student Orientation.

For more information about transcript submission guidelines, please visit the Submitting Official High School and College Transcripts Guidelines webpage.
