Unsubscribe

If you're no longer interested in hearing from the University of Houston, please follow the steps below.

  • Prospective Students
    If you don't have an application on file, you can unsubscribe by selecting the "Contact Preferences" link at the bottom of your last official UH communication. If you need assistance, call us at 713-743-1010, option 4.
  • Applicants & Admitted Students
    If you have an application on file, you can unsubscribe by completing the form linked below. If you've already been admitted, please check your myUH account for any outstanding fees.
