How to Register with the Dart Center

Step One

In order to register with the Dart Center, complete the new student application in the online student AIM portal.

Step Two

Submit relevant disability documentation through the student portal. Confirmation email will be sent.

Step Three

Schedule an appointment with an Access Coordinator to discuss and connect with accommodations. Please have your University of Houston Identification number, email address, and current contact information.

Step Four

Once registered, login to the student AIM portal each semester to submit accommodation request.

Current Students

Your approved accommodations may be accessed using our online portal under the ‘My Eligibility’ tab. SERVICES & RESOURCES

 

Interpreting and Captioning Services

The Student Accessibility Center at the University of Houston will be coordinating interpreting or captioning services for you. Learn more by visiting our Interpreting and Captioning Services webpage to learn more.

Become a Notetaker

Serving as a volunteer note-taker for a classmate with a disability. View our short guide for instructions on how to complete your registration with our office. Submit your application as a volunteer student note-taker.

Resources

The Dart Center takes great pride in partnering with campus and community to ensure students know what is available to them at the University of Houston. View our Academic and Support Resources page to learn more.

FAQs

The Dart Center has provided a variety of different topics for students and community members to learn about the different services, supports, registration process, accommodations, and other general questions about what is available. View our Frequently Asked Questions page to learn more.
