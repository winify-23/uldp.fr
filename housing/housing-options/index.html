Housing Options

Living on campus isn't just one experience or one community. It can be anything from sharing a suite with neighbors from around the world to Greek living in upscale apartments to urban lofts.

Need help making the decision?

  • Tour the Residence Halls through the UH Campus Experience Tour in person.
  • Take a virtual tour of our residence halls.
  • Compare Residences with our handy feature-by-feature chart.
  • See our Housing Rates.
