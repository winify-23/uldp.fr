Living Learning Communities (LLC)

LLCs enhance students’ on-campus living experience by offering an additional level of support which focuses on student success. Students will interact with and live among students involved in similar academic and co-curricular activities. We are dedicated to making our program an enriching learning environment for all of those who participate.

  1. UH Home
  2. Student Housing & Residential Life
  3. Living Learning Communities

ABOUT

A University of Houston Living Learning Community (LLC) is a group of students living together based on a common interest. Living Learning Communities support high levels of interaction between students who participate in and contribute to an immersive, year-long experience.

To apply for an LLC, you must have submitted your 2026 - 2027 Housing Agreement.

Current Communities

 

  • Bauer House

    In the Bauer House, you'll dive into engaging academic and social programming, enjoy
    collaborative study nights, and bond with fellow students at exciting College of Business sponsored events. It’s all about making meaningful connections and enhancing your college experience! Bauer House is available as a first-year LLC in Cougar Village I. The second-year LLC is located in The Quad. Visit the myHousing Portal to apply.

  • STEM LLC

    The STEM LLC is for first year students pursuing a degree in STEM. As part of this community, you’ll connect with faculty, explore career paths, participate in hands–on learning, and enjoy fun social events. This LLC is located in Cougar Village II. Visit the myHousing Portal to apply.

  • Cougar Experience Scholars

    Student success is the University of Houston’s biggest priority, and the Cougar Experience Scholarship (CES) was created for students who strive to make campus a more vibrant environment. The CES covers part of the cost of living on-campus. Awardees live together on a designated floor in Cougar Village II and intentionally get involved in various aspects of campus life. The living-learning community promotes campus engagement, lifelong friendships, and academic success. Visit the Cougar Experience Scholarship page to learn more.

  • Cougars in Recovery

    The Cougars in Recovery LLC is for students who choose to live a sober life. Students who reside in the Cougars in Recovery LLC are in recovery and are committed to continuing this lifestyle through college. Community members engage in productive campus groups and activities. Students who live in this community must be actively participating in a 12-step program and committed to participating in the floor community. The Cougars in Recovery LLC is housed in Bayou Oaks. To apply to live in this community, email Ellie Morris at mmorris6@central.uh.edu.

  • First Generation Student

    The First-Generation Student LLC, supported by the Challenger Program and located in Cougar Village I, offers first-year, first-generation students a supportive community that promotes academic success, personal growth and a strong sense of belonging at the University of Houston. Visit the myHousing Portal to apply.

  • Honors College

    Approximately 30 percent of Honors College freshmen choose to live on campus. By living in the Honors College LLC, students will have the opportunity to be a part of a close-knit community by way of informal gatherings, social activities, and cultural events. Students must be accepted into the Honors College or be a National Merit Scholar, Terry Scholar, or a Tier-One student to reside in Honors College LLCs. After admittance, students self-select to participate in the Honors College LLCs through the Housing Portal. The Honors College LLCs are housed in Cougar Village I and Cougar Place. For more information visit the Honors College website.

  • Impact

    Impact members focus on a variety of social issues throughout the year including topics such as hunger, homelessness, health, wellness, education, sustainability, and the effect they have on Houstonians. As members explore each social issue, they are challenged to learn how to think globally and act locally to have a positive impact on their community. Throughout their exploration of these social issues, Impact members will have the opportunity to participate in service related to the social issues discussed. Visit the myHousing Portal to apply.

  • Texas Leadership Scholars

    This living-learning community is for students who have been awarded the Texas Leadership Scholarship. Students will live and learn together in an academically and socially supportive community. For more information click here.

  • Global House

    Engage with a vibrant community of Global Coogs in UH’s Global House. Residents will have opportunities to connect with students from various countries and cultural backgrounds through social events, shared meals, discussions, faculty mentorship, and global competence focused workshops. This community offers an energetic environment for students to strengthen their global competence and get plugged into the many global engagement opportunities at UH and beyond. For more information, visit the Institute for Global Engagement website.
