SPIRIT TEAMS

The Spirit of Houston Spirit Teams are the spark of Cougar pride! Cheer, Dance & Mascots bring the energy that lights up every game, rally & celebration. With bold stunts, sharp moves & iconic Cougar spirit, they keep the crowd on fire and the campus buzzing. Whether on the field, the court or in the community, they represent the heart of UH with passion, strength & school pride.

CHEER

University of Houston cheerleaders will attend the following sporting events:

  • All home football games
  • Away football games
  • Men’s and Women’s basketball games
  • Men’s and Women’s basketball tournaments
  • Women’s volleyball games, Men’s Baseball games, and Women’s Softball Games

While our main priority is to support our athletic program, we also participate in many community outreach events. The Spirit of Houston cheer team typically goes to 175+ appearances including games, pep rallies, and community events. At the end of your college career, every cheerleader will be able to walk away to from not only a degree that will guarantee them a job, but with pride, friendships and memories that will last a lifetime. 

  • All applicants must be considered full time students at The University of Houston Main campus. Incoming freshman and transfer students will need to show proof of acceptance to UH before tryouts. 
  • Tryouts are by invite only, all non-returning applicants will need to complete these three things to be eligible for an invitation.
    • Proof of Acceptance to the University of Houston Main Campus 
    • Skills Requirement Video
    • Coaches Interview: 
      • Please follow UH Cheer on social media for audition deadlines and tryout information. For questions, please email the UH Cheer Staff at cheer@uh.edu.
  • Video Submission: A link to an unlisted youtube video will need to be submitted to Cheer@uh.edu by ____ @ 11:59 PM (Skills in the videos do not need to match Tryout Requirements, you should show off the best you have in this video.)
    • Standing Tumbling:
      • 3x standing back tucks with no cuts in the video 
      • 1x standing 2 BHS to highest skill, 1x Toe back, 1x Cartwheel back ○
    • Running Tumbling: 
      • 2 different passes with at least 3 skills ending in your best skill (i.e. round off BHS BHS Layout)
    • Stunting: 
      • All Girl: 3 Stunt variations that include at least a full up, full down & a body position. (i.e. heel stretch, arabesque, etc.)
      • Coed: 3 Stunts that show proficiency in partner stunting. (i.e. Full ups, Full downs, arabesque, heel stretches, etc.)

DANCE

The Cougar Dolls Dance Team is an integral part of the Spirit of Houston. Their main purpose is to engage and entertain fans at University of Houston Athletic Events as well as provide support for the University of Houston across the campus and city.

The Cougar Dolls are a team of well-rounded athletes who serve as the dance team within the Spirit of Houston, and can be seen performing a variety of styles at Cougar Football, Men’s Basketball, Women’s Basketball, Baseball and Women’s Volleyball games. The Cougar Dolls do not have a set number of team members that will make up the team each year. Traditionally, the team consists of 18-24 members. This number may fluctuate from year to year given the needs of the team. Team members are responsible for serving as positive, enthusiastic, and respectful ambassadors for The University of Houston, the Houston community, and other various platforms. Being a member of our team comes with several exciting opportunities but is also a major commitment and requires an extraordinary work ethic.

  • Tryouts are by invite only, all non-returning applicants will need to complete these three things to be eligible for an invitation.
    • Proof of Acceptance to the University of Houston Main Campus
    • Skills Requirement Video
    • Coaches Interview

1. Current University of Houston students or transfer students must have an overall 2.0 GPA.

2. Incoming freshmen must be accepted to the University of Houston. This is non-negotiable; register as early as possible to eliminate issues with delayed acceptance to the University of Houston.

3. Online registration and first round videos are live from March 26 - April 16. Online registration can be found here, and will open on March 26.

4. The final round of auditions will take place on May 4, 2025.

5. If you make the team, you are required to attend our pre-season camps and Spirit Week. Summer school class schedules will be accommodated.
