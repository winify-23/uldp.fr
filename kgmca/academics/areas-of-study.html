ApplySchools

At the Kathrine G. McGovern College of the Arts, we believe arts education empowers students to create, innovate, and lead across disciplines. From visual arts to music, theatre, dance, and more, our programs cultivate bold thinkers and makers. Keep exploring to see how you can shape the future through the arts.
