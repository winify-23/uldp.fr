WAYS OF GIVING

Create a lasting legacy that supports students for generations to come. With a gift of $50,000 or more, you can establish a permanent scholarship fund, where the principal invested and annual earnings provide ongoing support for students year after year. Name it for your family, company, or in honor of someone special. It’s a meaningful tribute that endures and grows over time, helping future students meet their aspirations.

Sustain the creative work that brings our campus and community to life. With a gift of $50,000 or more, you can create an endowed fund that provides ongoing support for performances, exhibitions, residencies, and academic programs across KGMCA. Your investment helps bring renowned artists, compelling works, and impactful experiences to students and audiences alike. A named fund offers a lasting way to celebrate your passion or honor someone meaningful to you.

Make an immediate difference with flexible support that addresses today's most urgent needs. Your annual gift funds scholarships, special projects, and emerging opportunities across the college. Designate your gift to any specific department, program, or the Dean's Excellence Fund for college-wide priorities and student support.

Give the college maximum flexibility to place resources where they're needed most. Unrestricted gifts allow us to respond quickly to opportunities, support students in crisis, fund innovative initiatives, and invest in equipment and supplies that enhance the learning experience.

Double or triple your impact through your employer's matching gift program. Simply complete your company's matching gift form and submit it with your donation. Contact your HR department to see if your organization participates. The matching funds will follow your original gift.

Honor an individual or family with a naming opportunity or planned gift. Create a lasting legacy through memorial funds or estate planning that ensures your values continue to shape future artists and performers.

Events That Inspire

Experience the vibrant community that makes the McGovern College so special through our signature fundraising events. From intimate artist receptions to our grand College Gala, each gathering brings together arts supporters, students, faculty, and Houston's creative and cultural leaders. 

Join us at the annual Moores School Luncheon for world-class student performances, celebrate at our elegant College Gala featuring all our programs, or connect with visiting artists at exclusive gallery openings with behind-the-scenes access. 

Every event combines exceptional artistry with meaningful support for scholarships, faculty excellence, and innovative programs. Whether you prefer intimate conversations or grand celebrations, our events create lasting memories while making a lasting impact.
