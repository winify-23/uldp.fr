YOUR ARTS DESTINATION

At the University of Houston's College of the Arts, creativity is always in motion. From thought-provoking exhibitions to world-class performances, our Arts District is where inspiration meets opportunity. Here's what to know before you arrive.

TICKETS & BOX OFFICE

Online: https://kgmcaboxoffice.universitytickets.com/ (recommended) 
View Calendar of Events: https://uh.edu/kgmca/explore/events
Phone: 713-743-3388 
Email: kgmcaboxoffice@uh.edu  

Monday–Friday, 9:00 a.m. – 2:00 p.m.  
Please note: Walk-up service is at the School of Theatre and Dance Box Office. Tickets can also be purchased starting one hour before a performance at the venue.

UH Students, Faculty & Staff

  • $15–$20 All Other McGovern College Events  
  • FREE for Moores School of Music Ensemble Performances
    • Students: Up to 2 free tickets per event
    • Faculty & Staff: Up to 4 free tickets per event
    • Must reserve at least 2 hours before the performance
    • How to Reserve:
      • Visit kgmcaboxoffice.universitytickets.com
      • Select “Purchase Tickets” under Moores School of Music
      • Change category to "Moores School of Music"
      • Choose the appropriate "UH Student" or "UH Faculty/Staff" ticket type
      • Register using your Cougarnet email to verify eligibility

UH Theatre & Dance Students

  • Free admission to productions you are performing in or working on
    • Contact the Production Stage Manager directly
    • Submit request at least one week before opening  

General Admission & Other Discounts

  • UH Alumni: $15–$20
  • Seniors (65+): $15–$20
  • General Public: $20–$25 

  • Door sales available for most events (first come, first served)  
  • Sold out online? Try our standby option: 
    • Cash-only standby tickets 60 minutes before curtain & only at venue (no phone calls for standbys) 
    • Wait in lobby 30 minutes before show 
    • Seated based on availability in order of purchase 
    • No guarantee of seating together or seat preference

PARKING INSTRUCTIONS

UH Permit Holders:

  • Park in designated permitted zone or garage.
  • Closest areas to the Arts District: Zone B, Elgin Garage, and Zone A.
  • Game Days: Permit holders may park in ungated lots: Zone A (17A), Zone C, and Zone D (details: Game Day Parking Info).

Visitors:

  • Elgin Street Garage (Visitor Section): Take a ticket and pay upon exit.
    • Rates: Visitor Parking Rates
  • Zone A and Zone B (Visitor Spaces):
    • Instructions: Pay by Phone
      Rates: Visitor Parking Rates
  • Game Days (details: Game Day Parking Info)
    • Weekends: All ungated lots are converted to pay-by-phone spaces at 6am - $30
    • Weekdays: All ungated lots are converted to pay-by-phone spaces 3 hours prior to game time - $30
  • UH Parking Maps: https://uh.edu/maps/

Until Zone B is Gated:

  • Onsite attendant at Zone B – $10 flat rate.

Once Zone B is Gated:

  • Prepaid entry with QR code scan – $10.
  • Onsite options - $12:
    • Elgin Street Garage: Take ticket and pay upon exit
    • Zone A - Soccer Field and Zone B (Visitor Spaces): Pay by phone visitor parking

Game Day Event Parking:

Unique parking instructions will apply and will be communicated in advance to attendees. Please refer to event-specific emails or announcements for the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding parking and access.

Additional lots that may be used with prior guest notification: 

  • Lot 18A
  • Lot 19E 

ACCESSIBILITY & VISITOR POLICIES

All venues are wheelchair and scooter accessible, with options to remain in your device or transfer to a theatre seat. 

To request accessible seating: 

  • Call 713-743-3388 when booking 
  • Available online or by phone

  • Plan to arrive at least 30 minutes early for parking and seating 
  • Mobile tickets are accepted — no need to print 
  • Digital programs are available via QR code on site 

  • Children ages 6 and older are welcome 
  • No food, drinks, photography, or recording inside venues 
  • Please silence all phones before performances 
  • Dress comfortably — we want you to enjoy the experience 

Quintero Theatre and Studio 208:

  • No late seating permitted 
  • Re-entry allowed at intermission only

Wortham Theatre & Moores Opera House:

  • Late seating available with usher assistance 
  • Temporary seating provided until intermission 

Note: Once performances begin, tickets are non-refundable and cannot be exchanged.
