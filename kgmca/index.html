Houston’s Arts Destination

Support the ArtsMessage from the Dean

Welcome to the Kathrine G. McGovern College of the Arts — home to fearless artists, world-class faculty, and boundary-breaking ideas shaping the creative future.

    IN THE HEART OF HOUSTON, AT THE EDGE OF WHAT’S NEXT.

    We are redefining what’s possible in the arts through bold ideas, collaboration, and a deep connection to our city.

    At the Kathrine G. McGovern College of the Arts,

    we are proud to be a premier institution for education, scholarship, and innovation in the arts. We are dedicated to cultivating the next generation of creative professionals and unlocking the transformative power of the arts to shape society and inspire change.

    Located in the heart of Houston’s historic Third Ward, KGMCA serves as a catalyst for exploration and transformation through its academic programs, performing arts centers, museum, and interdisciplinary spaces. Our students and faculty advance the future of the arts through bold experimentation, focused study, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

    As one of the nation’s leading cities for the arts, Houston provides a dynamic environment rich with opportunities for creative collaboration and professional growth.

    This vibrant cultural landscape, along with its extensive network of professionals and institutions, offers KGMCA students and faculty unparalleled resources to innovate and contribute meaningfully both locally and beyond.

    Rooted in both tradition and progress, KGMCA values the mastery of craft alongside the continual reimagining of creative practice. We view the arts as a vital means of exploring ideas, fostering connection, and deepening our engagement with the complexities of the world.

    Make Art That
    Moves the World

    At the Kathrine G. McGovern College of the Arts, we invite scholars, artists, performers, and creative thinkers committed to advancing the future of the arts. Whether your focus is in visual art, performance, design, or interdisciplinary research, you will join a community that challenges conventions, fosters critical inquiry, and supports your artistic and academic growth. Here, creativity, discipline, and innovation converge—begin your journey with us.

    Join the Alumni Association 

    The Kathrine G. McGovern College of the Arts Alumni Association is a vibrant community where the creative spirits of the Moores School of Music, the School of Art, and the School of Theatre & Dance beautifully intertwine.

    Our alumni association goes beyond celebrating the arts; it embraces the entire spectrum of the University of Houston’s rich traditions, proudly welcoming and including all members from the Spirit of Houston. Together, we foster pride and connection among all who make the UH Cougar experience unforgettable.

    Whether you’ve performed on our stages, exhibited your work in our galleries, led crowds in cheers, or marched with the band, you have a place here. Welcome to a community that celebrates every facet of our shared UH heritage.

    Research Excellence
    in the Arts

    In 2024, faculty in the McGovern College of the Arts premiered work around the world, earned national and international recognition, and published with leading academic and artistic platforms. Their contributions—spanning performance, research, curation, and community engagement—demonstrate the depth and reach of our creative scholarship.

    2024 Impact at a Glance:

    • 439 performances, exhibitions & productions
    • 47 publications across the visual arts, music, theatre & interdisciplinary research
    • $488,000+ awarded in grants and fellowships
    • Faculty presented work in 20+ countries
    • Featured in media, galleries & lecture halls worldwide
