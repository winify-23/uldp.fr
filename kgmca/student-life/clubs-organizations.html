Visit UsCampus Connections

At the Kathrine G. McGovern College of the Arts, student life extends far beyond the classroom. Whether you're looking to build community, explore leadership, or make a difference, there are plenty of ways to get involved and stay engaged. From arts-focused student groups to college-wide leadership opportunities and on-campus employment, your participation helps shape the creative culture of our college. 

How to Find Student Organizations

Connect with Campus-Wide Opportunities

Looking for a student organization that fits your interests? Explore the full list of active student groups across the University of Houston through the Center for Student Involvement. 

Explore the full list here:  Find Student Organizations at UH » 

KGMCA Clubs & Organizations

Create, Collaborate, and Connect 

KGMCA is home to a range of student groups focused on artistic disciplines and creative collaboration. These organizations host events, workshops, and performances—creating space for students to build community, share work, and deepen their craft. 

Highlighted groups include: 

  • BAMSA – Blaffer Art Museum Student Association 
  • DSA – Dance Student Organization 
  • Encore Theatre Company – Student-produced theatrical works 
  • Mariachi Pumas – Music performance and cultural outreach 
  • ALASA – Arts Leadership Alumni and Student Association 
    • Contact: UHartsleaders@gmail.com  

  • Kappa Kappa Psi 
  • Phi Mu Alpha Sinfonia 
  • Sigma Alpha Iota 
  • Tau Beta Sigma 

Amplifying Student Voice at the College Level 

The Strategic Initiatives Working Group is composed of students from across the college who collaborate with KGMCA leadership on projects, policy feedback, and community-building ideas. It’s an opportunity to lead change, connect with peers from all disciplines, and help shape the student experience. 

Contact: Associate Dean Fleurette S. Fernando fsfernando@uh.edu 

Partnering with the Dean’s Office 

This board brings together undergraduate and graduate students from each school to provide direct input to the Dean on academic life, facilities, policies, and student needs. Meetings occur once per semester and offer students a chance to be heard and involved in decision-making. 

Details: 

  • The Council includes up to 12 students, undergraduate and graduate, selected by competitive application.  
  • Members serve for one academic year (fall and spring semesters).  
  • Online applications will be open to full-time students at the beginning of each fall semester.  
  • Applicants must be enrolled in the McGovern College of the Arts for at least one semester before appointment to the Council and must not be on academic or disciplinary probation while serving on the Council.  
  • The Council meets monthly, and members must commit to regular attendance at meetings.  
  • Meetings are breakfast meetings with the Dean at 8:00 AM for one hour, on the first Tuesday of every month from October through May, except for January. 
  • The Council undertakes a service project for the entire College once each year. 

Represent the College. Inspire Your Peers. 

Ambassadors are passionate, engaged students who represent KGMCA at recruitment events, tours, orientations, and public programs. They offer peer-to-peer insight, help welcome new students, and play a vital role in outreach and engagement. 

Lead Within Your Discipline 

In addition to the college-wide program, each school may appoint student representatives who support recruitment, student engagement, and academic events within their departments. 

Work Where You Learn 

KGMCA offers student employment across departments and units, from box office and gallery support to marketing and event production. Work-study and non-work study positions help students build valuable experience while staying connected to the life of the college.
