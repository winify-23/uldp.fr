New Faculty Checklist

All new faculty coming to the University of Houston will receive individualized checklists to complete the required onboarding tasks. The table below is a general overview of departments for faculty onboarding. Your individualized checklist will provide you with more information and contacts.

Step Item Instructions For Questions, Contact:
1 Review your personalized checklist

Please review your personalized checklist and ensure we have your current email address. If you have any questions, contact the Office of Faculty Engagement and Development at 713- 743-7809.

If you are in the process of completing your Ph.D., you must show evidence of completion by August 1st. Please review the Transcript Guide. Faculty Affairs
