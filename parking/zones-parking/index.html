Zone Parking

The ungated parking lots on campus are broken down into different zones. Zone B will be gated starting in the fall 2025 semester. Zone permits can be purchased by students, faculty and staff.

Zone A comprises lots 16D, 16E, 17A, 17C and 17D on the north side of campus.

  • Close to: General Services Building, Houston Public Media, Schroeder Park, Cullen Intramural Fields
  • Google Street View Lot 16D
  • Google Street View Lot 16E
  • Do NOT park in restricted spaces, including reserved, disabled, 20-minute unloading and the orange text-to-pay spaces. If you park in a pay-by-phone space with your permit, you must pay.
  • Permit Cost: $257.50 per semester for students / $42.92 per month for faculty/staff (rates reflect 2025-2026 academic year)

Zone B is located on the north side of campus, just south of Elgin Street, and comprises lot 16B.

  • Close to: Blaffer Art Museum, Moores Opera House, Moores School of Music, Wilhelmina's Grove, Gerald D. Hines College of Architecture and Design, Jack J. Valenti School of Communication, Fertitta Center
  • Google Street View Lot 16B (view 1)
  • Google Street View Lot 16B (view 2)
  • Do NOT park in restricted spaces, including reserved, disabled, 20-minute unloading and the orange text-to-pay spaces. If you park in a pay-by-phone space with your permit, you must pay.
  • Permit Cost: $300 per semester for students / $50 per month for faculty/staff (rates reflect 2025-2026 academic year)

Lots 21A, 19C, 18A, 19D, and 21B make up Zone C, which is near University Lofts and the UH Law Center.

  • Close to: University Lofts, UH Law Center, Melcher Hall, Cemo Hall, Durga and Sushila Agrawal Engineering Research Building, Student Center North and South, University Classroom and Business Building, Cullen College of Engineering 1 and 2
  • Do NOT park in restricted spaces, including reserved, disabled, 20-minute unloading and the orange text-to-pay spaces. If you park in a pay-by-phone space with your permit, you must pay.
  • Permit Cost: $300 per semester for students / $50 per month for faculty/staff (rates reflect 2025-2026 academic year)

Zone D is on the southeast side of campus and includes ungated lots 4A, 3B, 3A, 5C, 5D, 5E and 6C.

  • Close to: Bayou Oaks, Moody Towers, Moody Towers Dining Commons, Health 1, Health 2, Welcome Center, South Park Annex
  • Google Street View Lot 4A (view 1)
  • Google Street View Lot 4A (view 2)
  • Google Street View Lot 3B
  • Do NOT park in restricted spaces, including reserved, disabled, 20-minute unloading and the orange text-to-pay spaces. If you park in a pay-by-phone space with your permit, you must pay.
  • Permit Cost: $257.50 per semester for students / $42.92 per month for faculty/staff (rates reflect 2025-2026 academic year)

The largest zone on campus is Zone E, located south of Wheeler Avenue. It includes lots 8A, 9C, 8B, 9B, and 10A.

  • Close to: Cullen Oaks, Cambridge Oaks, Quadrangle, Cougar Woods Dining Commons, Cameron Building, Clinical Research Services Center, Student Services Center 1, Student Services Center 2, Cougar Villages I and II, Justin Dart Jr. Center for Students with Disabilities
  • Google Street View Lot 8A (view 1)
  • Google Street View Lot 8A (view 2)
  • Google Street View Lot 9C (view 1)
  • Google Street View Lot 9C (view 2)
  • Do NOT park in restricted spaces, including reserved, disabled, 20-minute unloading and the orange text-to-pay spaces. If you park in a pay-by-phone space with your permit, you must pay.
  • Permit Cost: $257.50 per semester for students / $42.92 per month for faculty/staff (rates reflect 2025-2026 academic year)

Zone F comprises lot 12A, 15C, and 11B near TDECU Stadium.

  • Close to: TDECU Stadium, Cougar Place, UH Police Department, Cougar Woods Dining Commons, McElhinney Hall, Heyne Buiding, Fleming Building, Science Teaching Laboratory Building, Child Care Center, A.D. Bruce Religion Center, Farish Hall, R. Cullen Building, Garrison Gym and the Fertitta Center.
  • Google Street View Lot 12A (view 1)
  • Google Street View Lot 12A (view 2)
  • Do NOT park in restricted spaces, including reserved, disabled, 20-minute unloading and the orange text-to-pay spaces. If you park in a pay-by-phone space with your permit, you must pay.
  • Permit Cost: $257.50 per semester for students / $42.92 per month for faculty/staff (rates reflect 2025-2026 academic year)

Zone G comprises the ungated parking at the College of Medicine.

  • Close to: College of Medicine
  • Do NOT park in restricted spaces, including reserved, disabled, 20-minute unloading and the orange text-to-pay spaces. If you park in a pay-by-phone space with your permit, you must pay.
  • Permit Cost: $257.50 per semester for students / $42.92 per month for faculty/staff (rates reflect 2025-2026 academic year)

Zone H comprises the parking lots at the University of Houston Technology Bridge.

  • Close to: University of Houston Technology Bridge
  • The covered parking locations require a Zone H Covered Parking Permit.
  • Do NOT park in restricted spaces, including reserved, disabled, 20-minute unloading and the orange text-to-pay spaces. If you park in a pay-by-phone space with your permit, you must pay.
  • Permit Cost: $257.50 per semester for students for uncovered parking / $42.92 per month for faculty/staff uncovered and $44.83 per month for covered (rates reflect 2025-2026 academic year)
  • Students are not eligible to purchase a permit to park in the covered parking areas of Zone H.

Which Zone
Is Best For You?

The eight parking zones are located in different parts of campus. Students deciding which zone to select can use the following guide to help them make the choice that works the best for them.

Nearest by College

Zone A

  •  Nearest to colleges listed for Zone B (Zone A is just north of Zone B)

Zone B

  • Gerald D. Hines College of Architecture and Design
  • Kathrine G. McGovern College of the Arts
  • College of Natural Sciences and Mathematics
  • Graduate College of Social Work
  • College of Technology

Zone C

  • C.T. Bauer College of Business
  • Cullen College of Engineering
  • UH Law Center
  • Hobby School of Public Affairs

Zone D

  • Conrad N. Hilton College of Global Hospitality Leadership
  • College of Optometry
  • College of Pharmacy

Zone E

  • Nearest to the colleges listed for Zone F

Zone F

  • College of Education

Note: The various departments within the College of Liberal Arts and Social Sciences are located in different parts of campus. For help deciding which zone is closest to each of the CLASS departments, contact Parking and Transportation Services at parking@uh.edu or 832-842-1097.
