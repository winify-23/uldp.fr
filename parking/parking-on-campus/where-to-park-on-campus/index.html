Where to Park on Campus

Anyone who brings a vehicle to the University of Houston campus should be aware of where to park before arriving. Students, faculty, staff and visitors can find information about where they should park on this webpage. Anyone who has questions about where to park can email parking@uh.edu or call 832-842-1097.

Parking Permit Holders

Students, faculty and staff who have a valid University of Houston parking permit must park in the location specified by their permit Monday-Thursday between 7 a.m. and 3 p.m. .

If it is Monday-Thursday after 3 p.m., or any time of the day Friday, Saturday or Sunday, then most valid UH parking permit holders can also park in any of the ungated/uncovered zone parking lots on campus, in addition to their designated location. There are several exceptions to this rule:

  • Evening-Weekend permits can only be used to park on campus after 3 p.m. Monday-Friday (and any time on Saturday and Sunday).
  • Tuesday-Thursday and Monday-Wednesday-Friday permits can only be used to park in their designated garage and only on the days designated by the permit.
  • Katy permits can only be used at the University of Houston at Katy campus.
  • Do not park in restricted areas such as fire and loading zones, disabled or reserved spaces, which are enforced 24 hours a day, seven days a week.
  • If you park in an orange pay-by-phone space, you must pay the visitor rate, even if you have a valid UH parking permit.

Visitors / Those Without a Parking Permit

Anyone who comes to campus without a valid University of Houston parking permit must pay to park in any of the visitor parking locations on campus.

There are two types of visitor parking on campus: the visitor sections of most of the parking garages, and the pay-by-phone spaces marked with orange striping in ungated parking lots and the Welcome Center North Garage.

The visitor parking rates are posted online.

Parking for Athletic Events

Cougar Football

  • UH students, faculty and staff can find detailed parking information for football game days on the Campus Parking on Game Day page.
  • Visitors coming to campus to attend a UH Cougar football game can park in the locations specified on the Athletics football parking page.

Baseball

  • Fans driving to campus to attend a Cougar baseball game at Schroeder Park should park in the locations specified by UH Game Day Operations.

Softball

  • Fans driving to campus to attend a Cougar softball game should park in the locations specified by UH Game Day Operations.

Cullen Performance
Hall Event Parking

Cullen Performance Hall hosts many shows, concerts and performances every year. Visitors coming to campus to attend one of these shows can find detailed parking information on the Cullen Performance Hall website.

Parking Locations

University Commencement has this webpage set up with parking information for visitors, including a commencement parking map.

New Student Orientations

Students and their families who come to campus to attend orientation will be sent specific parking instructions one week before their orientation and again the day before.

Residential Move-In

Students in any of the residential communities on campus should consult the Residential Housing and Student Life website for details on where they should and should not park when moving in.
