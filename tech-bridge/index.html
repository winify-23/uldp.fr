Startup Development at Technology Bridge

Our Innov8Hub program guides founders through every stage—Discovery → Planning → Funding—with structured support and expert mentorship to help turn potential into progress.

Strategy

Support to help you think through your startup’s direction and next steps:

  • Innov8Hub: guided path from idea to funding
  • Business model and customer discovery workshops
  • Pitch coaching and storytelling support
  • Strategic planning and milestone setting
Get Started

Resources

Practical tools and access points to help you build and test your ideas:

  • Access to UH labs and prototyping tools
  • Shared workspace and meeting rooms
  • Office hours with legal, financial, and technical advisors
  • Market research and grant opportunity guidance

Community

Opportunities to connect, learn, and grow with others on a similar path:

  • Peer founder meetups and roundtables
  • Guest speaker events and panels
  • Introductions to UH faculty, alumni, and industry contacts
  • Social mixers and networking opportunities
