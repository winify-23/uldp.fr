Resources and Tools

  • UH Top Tier Benefits

    The University offers a comprehensive package of Top Tier Benefits designed to support the health and wellbeing of our employees. This includes extensive health benefits (medical, dental, and vision), optional term life insurance, dependent life insurance, voluntary accident & dismemberment insurance, and both short-term and long-term disability coverage. Employees also have access to TexFlex Flexible Spending Accounts (FSAs), retirement programs, paid holidays, vacation and sick leave, and a variety of wellness benefits to foster overall health and work-life balance.
