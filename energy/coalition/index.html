Preparing UH Students for Success in Energy

 
  1. UH Home

The Energy Coalition is the hub for energy-minded students at UH — a campus-wide network uniting thousands of students and their organizations across engineering, business, law, policy, and sustainability. 

We bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world energy careers by connecting students with industry leaders, academic partners, and hands-on opportunities. Whether you're new to energy or leading a student org, EC empowers you to collaborate, innovate, and lead the future of energy.

Future Energy Leaders

The Energy Coalition is the University of Houston’s largest energy-focused student organization. Sponsored by the The Energy UniversityTM, The EC unites over 50 student organizations and thousands of students interested in all aspects of the energy sector—from traditional oil and gas to renewables, tech, and policy. 

Meet our Board

From Campus to Career

We believe that the future of energy depends on the people we prepare today. The EC is more than a student organization—it’s a launchpad for leadership, a network of opportunity, and a hands-on learning environment. We believe that real growth happens when students are empowered to lead, collaborate across disciplines, and engage directly with the challenges and innovations shaping the energy sector.
