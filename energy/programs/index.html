Energy Education Programs

The Energy University® — where industry-driven learning shapes the future of energy.

At The Energy University®, we believe education is the engine driving the future of energy. Across every discipline — science, engineering, business, law, policy, technology — we are preparing students and professionals to lead the energy industry and thought leadership in a world transformed by digital innovation and AI.

The future of the energy workforce is full of opportunity:

  • 2.1 million new energy-related jobs projected by 2050, driven by an “all-of-the-above” strategy.
  • Major growth across hydrocarbons, renewables, liquid fuels, hydrogen, biomass, and carbon management.
  • Expanding demand for talent balancing sustainability, reliability, and affordability.

To meet this challenge, we offer:

  • Undergraduate programs that build strong scientific foundations and critical thinking.
  • Graduate and professional programs across science, engineering, technology, law policy and business.
  • Reskilling, upskilling, and continuing education designed to help today’s workforce thrive in a fast-changing industry shaped by digital and AI advances.

Standing still isn’t an option — and that’s where we come in: creating learning pathways that empower learners at every stage to drive innovation, solve global energy challenges, and deliver a reliable, affordable, sustainable, and smarter energy future for all.

Micro-Credentials 

Our micro-credential programs are designed for agility — created in direct response to advisory board input, they help working professionals quickly upskill in response to emerging industry gaps and needs. These targeted courses let you stay ahead without committing to a full degree, making sure your expertise remains sharp and relevant.

Why Study Abroad

Energy connects the world — and studying abroad lets you see that firsthand. Explore international systems, policies, and innovations, broaden your perspective, and build global connections. You'll return with insights that give you a competitive edge and help solve energy challenges that cross borders.
