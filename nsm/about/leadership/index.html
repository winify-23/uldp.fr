Dean’s Advisory Board

External advice and expertise is provided by the Dean’s Advisory Board, a distinguished group of scientists, physicians and industry representatives, many of whom are also alumni. These key volunteers, appointed by the dean, serve as strategic advisors providing key connections to the community, health care and industry.
