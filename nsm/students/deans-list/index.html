NSM Dean’s List & Dean’s Distinguished Scholars List

If you would prefer to have your name removed from the website, or if you find an error in your name as posted, please send an email to tsims4@uh.edu. Include your name, myUH ID, your major in the College of NSM, the list where your name is featured (Dean’s or Distinguished Scholars), and the term. NOTE: you must be a major in the College of NSM at the start of the term to have your name on the list.

NSM Dean’s List

To qualify for NSM Dean’s List recognition, undergraduate students must earn a 3.50 minimum grade point average (the grade of S is not counted) on 12 or more credit hours completed during the term. Typically, the list posts 4–6 weeks after the closing of the fall or spring term. Students who earn a grade of I (incomplete), D, F or U, during the term are excluded from consideration for the Dean’s List. Dean’s List recognition can also be found on a student’s official transcript.

NSM Dean’s Distinguished Scholars List

The NSM Dean’s Distinguished Scholars List is the highest honor for undergraduates. The designation was created by the College in Spring 2018. To be placed on this list, students must meet all requirements of the Dean’s List and also have both a term and cumulative GPA of 3.75. Typically, the list posts 4–6 weeks after the closing of the fall or spring term. This distinction does not appear on the official transcript. However, Dean’s List recognition will appear on a student’s official transcript.
