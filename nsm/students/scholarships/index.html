The College of Natural Sciences and Mathematics (NSM) encourages students to pursue studies in the sciences and mathematics through its scholarship program.

There are several scholarship opportunities available to NSM students. More information on the various scholarships and how to apply can be found on the following pages:

NSM Scholarship Program

Deadline: February 27, 2026, at 5 PM Central (application opens Nov 1)

Open to incoming freshmen and continuing students. A few scholarships are open to NSM graduate students.

More Info ›

Science and Math Mentoring Scholarship Program

Deadline: May 5, 2025

Open to undergraduate students who demonstrate financial need by qualifying for a Federal Pell Grant, as determined by completing the FAFSA, and have a declared major in the Departments of Earth and Atmospheric Sciences, Mathematics, or Physics.

More Info ›

teachHOUSTON Scholarship Program

Deadline: November 1, 2025, at 11:59 PM Central

Open to NSM undergraduate students in the teachHOUSTON program who have completed CUIN 2300.

More Info ›

STEM Scholarship Program

Deadline: August 13, 2024, at 5 PM Central

Open to sophomore and junior NSM majors.

More Info ›

NSM Alumni Association Scholarship Program

Deadline: April 15, 2025, at 11:59 PM Central

The UH Alumni Association Foundation offers the NSMAA Scholarship as well as other scholarship opportunities.

More Info ›
