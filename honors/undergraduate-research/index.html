Welcome to
OURMA

Promoting mentored undergraduate research and fellowship opportunities for students across the university

The Office of Undergraduate Research and Major Awards provides support, resources, and research opportunities for undergraduate students through faculty-mentored research programs. We also provide guidance for seeking out and applying to competitive fellowships and major awards for undergraduate and graduate students, as well as alumni.
