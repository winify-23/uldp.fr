Graduate Academic Advising

Graduate students are advised by both the faculty within their graduate program and a professional academic advisor. Faculty advisors direct students in curricular studies and research advancement, while the professional advising staff assists students with enrollment, student administration and paperwork. Below are the professional staff academic advisors for graduate programs on campus. Graduate students should maintain regular contact with both their faculty advisor and their academic advisor.

NOTE: Prospective students and applicants should make contact with the admissions staff contacts located on the Admissions Contacts page.
