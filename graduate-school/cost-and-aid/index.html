Graduate Costs & Aid

Graduate education is a valuable investment that can lead to new opportunities and career growth. At the University of Houston, we help you understand the costs and explore financial resources like scholarships, assistantships, and aid.

Tuition Calculator Types of Aid

Tuition & Fees

Tuition and fees vary depending on your department, your residence status, your graduate level and the courses you're taking.

Academic Year 2026 Tuition Calculator

Types of Aid

The University of Houston offers several different kinds of financial assistance, ranging from scholarships that are awarded on the basis of academic merit to loans awarded on the basis of financial need. A typical financial aid package includes more than one type of aid.
