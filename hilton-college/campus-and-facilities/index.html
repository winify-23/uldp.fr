Work Where You Learn

Work Where You Learn

Cougar Grounds Coffeehouse

Cougar Grounds is the first student-run coffeehouse on a U.S college campus and a hands-on learning lab for Hilton College students. From sourcing and staffing to marketing and operations, students manage every aspect of the business. With two busy locations, Cougar Grounds offers real-world experience in entrepreneurship and food and beverage leadership.

Work Where You Learn

The Hilton Hotel That Teaches

The Hilton University of Houston is a full-service teaching hotel where 70% of the staff are hospitality students. From front desk to banquets, students gain real-world experience across all departments. With 86 guest rooms, meeting spaces, and a full-service restaurant, the hotel serves as a dynamic training ground for future hospitality leaders.

Work Where You Learn

Eric's Restaurant

Located inside the Hilton University of Houston, Eric's is more than a restaurant-it's a hub for the UH community. Enjoy globally inspired, chef-driven dishes, student-crafted cocktails, and a warm, modern atmosphere. With Houston's first robot server, a refreshed menu, and patio dining. Eric's blends innovation with hospitality. Open daily for breakfast, lunch, dinner, and happy hour.

SPEC's Beverage & Food Appreciation Lab

This state-of-the-art lab offers hands-on training in beverage management, wine appreciation, and food pairing. With industry-standard equipment and expert instruction, students gain the expertise to lead in beverage operations.

Experiential Classrooms

SYSCO Student Kitchen

More than just a lab, this professional-grade kitchen serves as a learning ground for future chefs and food service leaders. Here, students practice culinary skills, develop recipes, and run mock operations in a real-world setting.

Hospitality Industry Archives

Step into the world's largest collection dedicated to preserving the story of hospitality. Housed in the Massad Family Library Research Center, the archives hold thousands of rare menus, photographs, documents, and memorabilia from industry icons like Marriott, Westin, and Howard Johnson. From Mad Men set designs to published features by the Smithsonian and New York's Waldorf Astoria, Hollywood productions, historians, and hospitality pros alike turn to our archives for authentic details that bring the past to life.
