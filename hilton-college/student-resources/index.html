Academic Advising

Your academic journey starts here. Our advising team supports undergraduate, graduate, and transfer students with degree planning, petitions, scholarships, study abroad referrals and graduation prep. Whether you're just getting started or preparing to cross the stage, we’re here to help you stay on track and thrive.

Massad Family Library Research Center

Our second-floor Massad Family Library offers a modern, welcoming space for research, study, and collaboration. With private meeting rooms, smart tech, dedicated workstations, and printing stations for GLH students, it’s where ideas take shape and careers begin.

Student Resources

Eric’s Club Center For Student Success

Your one-stop hub for academic advising, career counseling, and personalized student support. From application to graduation, our recruitment, academic services, and career development teams are here to guide you—starting at our convenient concierge desk.

Student Resources

The Planck Family Student Lobby

The student lobby is now more inviting and functional thanks to the Planck Family’s generosity and modern furniture from Vari. Their contribution enhances the student and guest experience and reflects the impact of giving on our inclusive, dynamic College community.

 

Get Involved: Organizations & Events

Find your community at Hilton College! From student orgs that build friendships and skills to traditions like Gourmet Night and Cougar Grounds, there’s always something to experience. Get involved or celebrate with us.

Student Organizations

Hilton College hosts nine student-led groups, including one for graduate students, offering ways to connect, give back, network, and gain real-world hospitality experience.

Traditions

From Gourmet Night to Cougar Grounds, Hilton College traditions bring students together to celebrate hospitality, build community, and create lasting memories.
