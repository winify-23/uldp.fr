Weaving History into Design

When Hines College student Marshall Bradley first learned he had been selected for the Project Row Houses’ Floyd Newsum Summer Studios Mentorship + Residency Program, he knew it was more than a creative opportunity—it was a chance to explore the deep history and humanity of Houston’s Third Ward. Now, after completing the residency and presenting his exhibition in August and September, Bradley reflects on a summer that not only transformed his perspective as a designer but also deepened his connection to the community he sought to honor.

Alumni Spotlight: Mariam Fallah (M.Arch. '17)

October 22, 2025

Alumni Spotlight: Mariam Fallah (M.Arch. '17)

For Hines College alumna Mariam Fallah (M.Arch. '17), architecture is rooted in collaboration and curiosity. Now a Principal at GSMArchitects, she brings that spirit to projects shaping communities across Houston, including recent work with the Houston Area Women’s Center. On her time at the College, she said, “The Hines College taught me the importance of dialogue and the value of sharing ideas with colleagues and friends ... Collaboration was always at the core.”

Alumni Spotlight: Sam D'Amico, FAIA (B.Arch. '81)

Sam D'Amico, FAIA (B.Arch. '81) is a proud Hines College alum who is inspired by the design and emotive aspects of architecture. As the national healthcare design director at SmithGroup, where he is also a vice president, he integrates art, nature, and daylight to create healing environments.
