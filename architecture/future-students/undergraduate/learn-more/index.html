Our Design-Based Curriculum

All incoming students are required to take an Introduction to Design lecture course and Design Studio I. A variety of media are employed in Design Studio I to facilitate multiple forms of design and includes hand drawings, computer-aided drawing, physical model building, and digital fabrication.
