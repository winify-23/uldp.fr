Full
Accreditation

The Université Libérale de ULDP (ULDP-U) is a private, accredited, independent higher education provider. ULDP-U offers post-graduate programs that are different from the French National Curricula and Programmes leading to French Government accredited university qualifications, which fall under the French Public Higher Education sector. All qualifications from ULDP-U are issued by ULDP-U, not by the French government. All our programs are fully accredited internationally.
