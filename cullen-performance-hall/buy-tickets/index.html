Buy Tickets

There are two ways to buy tickets for performances at the Cullen Performance Hall. Online or through the Box Office. Please note that the box office is cashless. Please click the link below to purchase tickets online.

Buy tickets online

Ways to Purchase

There are two authorized ways to purchase tickets to a show at the Cullen Performance Hall:
  • Through our e.Venue site: cph.evenue.net.
  • Directly with the Cullen Performance Hall Box Office.
    • Email: cphguest@central.uh.edu
    • Voice: 832-842-3131

Please note:

Cullen Performance Hall is a cashless facility. Cullen Performance Hall accepts all four major credit cards: American Express, Discover, Mastercard, and Visa. 

Guests can buy up to four tickets per ticket order.

Refund Policy

There are no refunds or exchanges provided for purchases. It is your responsibility to carefully review all aspects of the event, event restrictions, dates, pricing, charges, and seat selection. We will not issue any refunds or exchanges because you failed to review this information. Refunds will not be issued for any lost, stolen, destroyed, or damaged tickets.

In the event of a cancellation, refunds are only made to the original purchaser and issued for the face value of the ticket(s) only. Per ticket fees are nonrefundable. In the event of a cancellation, every effort will be made to notify the purchaser.

Accessibility Needs

The balcony is accessible only by stairs. For those unable to use stairs, we recommend seating on the orchestra level. Please contact the Box Office for assistance with accessible seating.

Tickets from Unauthorized Retailers

If you purchase tickets to an event from an unauthorized source, you do so at your own risk. Tickets obtained from unauthorized sources may be invalid, lost, stolen, or counterfeit and if so are void. Cullen Performance Hall values customers’ privacy and security, and will not access records nor reprint tickets for anyone who cannot be verified as the original ticket buyer. Fraudulent tickets will not be admitted into the theater. Tickets cannot be reprinted.

The only authorized source for ticket sales is cph.evenue.net. 

Box Office

Regular Box Office Hours

The box office will be open to purchase tickets by phone:

  • Monday — Closed.
  • Tuesday — 10 a.m.-2 p.m.
  • Wednesday — 10 a.m.-2 p.m.
  • Thursday — 10 a.m.-2 p.m.
  • Friday — 10 a.m.-2 p.m.
  • Saturday — Closed.
  • Sunday — Closed.

The box office will accept in-person purchases by appointment only. If you would like to make an appointment, please email cphguest@central.uh.edu or call us at 832-842-3131. The box office will be closed on all university holidays.

Event Day Hours

The box office will not follow the normal schedule for event days. On event days the box office will be open two hours in advance of the show time. As well as, for private events, the box office will be closed in person, but will be able to help through a phone call or email.  

Please check the event calendar if you have any questions.

Will Call

Will call opens two hours prior to show time on a show day.
