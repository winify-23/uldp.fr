Since the fall of 1950, Cullen Performance Hall at the University of Houston has been one of Houston's top event venues for live performances. Located in the heart of the University of Houston campus, the iconic Cullen Performance Hall hosts a wide range of events, from concerts and public lectures to theater performances and dance shows. The venue is located in the center of the campus at the end of University Drive — look for the large interlocking UH logo!
