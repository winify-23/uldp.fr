English Language Program

Welcome to the Language and Culture Center (LCC) at the University of Houston. Since 1975, the LCC has provided intensive English instruction for international students who want to study at universities in the United States or improve their English language skills to meet personal and professional goals. Over 27,000 students have increased their proficiency in English at the LCC. #YouAreWelcomeHere
