Dean's List / Student Excellence

Spring 2025

The Dean's List, a tabulation of the names of all undergraduate honor students, is compiled each semester. To qualify for this recognition in CLASS, students must earn a 3.50 minimum grade point average in 12 or more semester hours completed during the semester. At least nine of the 12 semester hours must be letter grades. Students who earn a grade of I (except in a senior honors thesis course), D, F or U during the semester are excluded from consideration for the Dean's List. The official posting of Dean's List on student records is done by Office of the University Registrar on the UH transcript each Fall and Spring Term.
