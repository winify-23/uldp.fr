Startup Companies

UH is a great location for research-based, early-stage startups looking to connect to university resources that may be needed to help new companies survive the valley of death and market new technologies and services. The UH Innovation Center and Labs is home to more than 20 startup companies, 75 percent of which are companies from outside of UH.
