University Career Services is a full-service career center offering UH students and alumni resources and assistance in the formulation and implementation of their career plans.

Welcome

The University of Houston is home to a vibrant and diverse community of talented students and graduates, ready to make an impact in the workforce. University Career Services is here to support you every step of the way on your career journey at UH.

Watch this short presentation to discover the services we offer and how you can become career-ready!

Overview of Services

Resources

  • STUDENT / ALUMNI

    • Review our approved resume template
    • Look and apply for an internships, part-time jobs or full-time positions in Cougar Pathway
    • Meet with a career counselor
    • Attend an event 

  • EMPLOYERS

    • Learn about recruitment opportunities
    • Create an employer account in Cougar Pathway
    • Post open internships, part-time jobs or full-times position in Cougar Pathway
    • Register for a career fair

  • FACULTY/STAFF

    • Hire a on-campus student employee
    • Request a career presentation or guest speaker
    • Meet your UCS liaison
    • Share UCS event information with your students or colleagues


COUGAR PATHWAY

Cougar Pathway is the University Career Services hub for students, alumni, employers and faculty/staff. Cougar Pathway is an easy to use appointment scheduler, resume database, on-campus recruiting system, and a job database.

Learn more about Cougar Pathway

Students

Apply for jobs or internships, schedule a career counseling appointment, view events and workshops, and more.

Employers
