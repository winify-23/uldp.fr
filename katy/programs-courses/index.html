Programs

From nursing to engineering, our pgraoms drive students to discover innovative solutions to real-world situations. Our Katy campus is home to programs from the Andy & Barbare Gessner College of Nursing and the Cullen College of Engineering, which partners with Houston Community College on the UH/HCC Engineering Academy.

Certificate Programs

Boost your skills and advance your career without leaving Katy. We offer a range of certification programs delivered 100% onsite, no commute required. Whether you are looking to gain new credentials or sharpen your expertise, explore convenient options designed to fit your schedule and keep you close to home.
