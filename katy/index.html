Engineering Excellence

The Cullen College of Engineering offers innovative and industry-relevant undergraduate and graduate level classes and certificate programs at UH's Katy campus conveniently located near Houston's Energy Corridor. 

Cullen College of Engineering

Top-Ranked Nursing

Pursue an in-demand undergraduate or graduate nursing degree through the Andy & Barbara Gessner College of Nursing, one of the nation's top-ranked programs. Students receive high quality instruction and hands-on training in our state-of-the-art facilities.

UH/HCC Engineering Academy

The UH/HCC Engineering Academy provides a lower cost pathway to a UH Engineering degree. The unique partnership between the Cullen College of Engineering and Houston Community College enables coenrolled students to complete their core curriculum at HCC and engineering courses at UH.
