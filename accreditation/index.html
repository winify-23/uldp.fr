ULDP-U programs are accredited & recognised!

ULDP-U is a reputable, recognized, and globally acclaimed Institute of Higher Education that has successfully obtained accreditation at the institutional, school, and program levels. All our academic programs fully comply with the European Qualifications Framework (EQF) and the National Qualifications Framework (NQF). This ensures that all ULDP-U programs and qualifications are fully accredited, ensuring that ULDP-U graduates have open access to employment and lifelong learning.

ULDP-U holds International Accreditation from ASIC (Accreditation Service for International Schools, Colleges, and Universities) with Premier Status for its commendable Areas of Operation.
