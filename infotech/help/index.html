Got a Question?

Or Search the UIT Help Knowledge Base

For computing, account or support issues

  • Manage your UIT support cases online
  • Chat with us on Live Chat
  • Emaiil us at uitsupport@uh.edu
  • Call or Text us at 713-743-1411
  • Visit us at at Tech Commons located at the back entrance of the MD Anderson Library, across from the Student Center (View map to Tech Commons) from 8:00 a.m. to 8 p.m., Monday through Friday (except university holidays).

For phone, data network or video issues

  • Manage your network work order cases online
  • Call Us: 713-743-1411
  • Fax us: 713-743-1109

Additional Email Addresses

security@uh.edu
To report illegal or questionable incidents, including phishing.

software@uh.edu

Classroom equipment questions and reservations.
www@uh.edu
For questions, comments or suggestions about the UH Web site.
