Policies & Guidelines

University Information Technology policies are published as University of Houston System Administrative Memoranda (SAMs) and Manual of Administrative Policies and Procedures (MAPPs).  All current UIT SAMs and MAPPs are listed below.  Visit the University of Houston Policies and Procedures site to view all policies currently in effect for the System and for the University of Houston.

IT SAMs

  • 07.A.03 - Responsibilities for Information Technology Resources
  • 07.A.04 - Digital Millenium Copyright Act
  • 07.A.07 - Use of Electronic Messaging Services by Employees
  • 07.A.08 - Data Classification and Protection
  • 07.A.10 - Information Security Program
  • 07.A.11 - Information Security Incident Reporting and Investigation

IT MAPPs

  • 10.03.01 - Acceptable Use of Information Resources
  • 10.03.04 - Connecting Devices to University Networks
  • 10.03.06 - College/Division Responsibilities for Information Technology Resources


UIT has published Central web server policies to provide information to users on the following: Account policies, graphics standards, registering the site to UH web page, administrative policies for UH Web server and URL Policies.
