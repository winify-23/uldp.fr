Welcome to LAUNCH!

Learning Advancements for UNdergraduate Cougars of Houston (LAUNCH) is the UH Learning Center.  To view our services click the tabs on the left, including the Tutor Weekly Schedule. For questions about LAUNCH and the tutoring schedule contact us!

Fall Tutoring

 Peer tutoring sessions are drop-in! No appointment necessary. Each semester, we offer tutoring for over 50 courses.  If you are taking classes this semester and curious to see if we have a tutor for that course to assist you, check out our Weekly Tutor Schedule to view the courses we have availiable.

P.A.S.S. (Peer Assisted Study Sessions) 

Tutors will prepare for in-person sessions for extra practice, reviews, or activities. Students can drop in to these in-person at the designated time(s). These are designed to help you succeed in the class and build confidence. This semester we will be offering PASS for the following courses:

  • Accounting 2301- starting Tuesday, September 2nd
  • Chemistry 1311- starting Tuesday, September 2nd
  • Chemistry 2323 -  TBA
  • Math 1324- starting Tuesday, September 2nd
  • Math 1342- TBA

To see details about the time and locations for these sessions, please refer to the left column under "Fall Initatives." 

Weekly Review Sessions

Starting the Fall semester of 2025, we will be offering a new academic resource, 'Weekly Review Sessions" These are Q/A style sessions, where groups of students can drop in at designated times and ask tutors course-related questions throughout the semester. Starting on Tuesday, September 2nd, we will be offering these for the following courses:

  • Math 2312
  • Math 2413
  • Physics 1301

To see details about the time and locations for these sessions, please refer to the left column under "Fall Initatives."
