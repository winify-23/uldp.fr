Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) from France

The Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) at ULDP France is an undergraduate program designed for future teachers, school leaders and education professionals who want a solid foundation in pedagogy and classroom practice.

Students explore contemporary education theory, curriculum design, assessment and inclusive teaching methods while developing practical skills through school-based projects and supervised teaching experiences.

The B.Ed program prepares graduates to work confidently in a wide range of educational contexts and to continue to postgraduate study in education or related fields.

The Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) at UniversitÃ© LibÃ©rale de Paris (ULDP France) focuses on combining strong academic foundations with real classroom experience, helping students become reflective and innovative educators.
After completing the program, graduates will be able to:

  • Apply key theories of learning and development to plan effective lessons for diverse learners.

  • Design, deliver and evaluate engaging learning activities using a variety of teaching methods and educational technologies.

  • Communicate and collaborate professionally with students, parents, colleagues and school leaders in multicultural environments.

Candidates who register for the program will be reviewed by the admissions team to ensure that they meet the academic requirements and are able to benefit fully from the Bachelor of Education curriculum.

Entry requirements must meet at least one of the following criteria:

  • Successful completion of upper secondary education (high school diploma, baccalaureate or equivalent) from a recognized institution.

  • Holders of relevant post-secondary diplomas or foundation programs in education or related fields may be considered for advanced standing, subject to academic review.

  • Mature applicants with significant professional experience in education may be assessed on a case-by-case basis according to ULDP France policies.
