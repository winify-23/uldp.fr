Bachelor of Education (BEd) - Top-Up

Home > Bachelor of Education
Share :
Upgrade your education

The Bachelor of Education - Top-Up is designed for working professionals who want to upgrade their education and skills in the field of education. The program is tailored to meet the needs of educators and is delivered in a flexible format that allows students to balance their work and study.

By joining the Bachelor of Education - Top-Up program, students will gain advanced knowledge and skills in education, as well as the ability to apply research and theoretical approaches to real-world problems in education.

Why This BEd Top-Up Matters

Unlike traditional three- or four-year undergraduate education degrees, the BEd Top-Up provides a direct and structured progression route for diploma holders and education professionals.

Participants engage with advanced educational theory, applied pedagogy, and professional teaching practice aligned with current educational standards and institutional expectations.

The programme emphasises teaching effectiveness, academic progression, and professional credibility, making it ideal for educators seeking both career advancement and further study opportunities.

Program Overview

The BEd Top-Up focuses on advanced undergraduate education studies and professional teaching competence. The curriculum is designed to bridge prior learning with bachelor-level expectations, preparing graduates for professional teaching roles and further academic progression in education-related fields.

Program Structure

Students complete advanced-level education modules and a final project designed to integrate theory with practical application in real educational contexts.

The programme emphasizes:

  • Teaching and learning theory

  • Curriculum design and instructional planning

  • Classroom management and learner engagement

  • Assessment, evaluation, and reflective practice

  • Research, analysis, and professional presentation skills

Learning Outcomes

Graduates of the Bachelor of Education (Top-Up) will be able to:

  • Apply educational theory to effective teaching practice

  • Design, deliver, and evaluate learning activities across diverse contexts

  • Demonstrate professional communication and classroom leadership skills

  • Conduct applied educational research and present professional projects

  • Progress confidently into teaching roles or postgraduate education study

Entry requirements

Applicants to the Bachelor of Education (Top-up) must meet the following criteria:

  • Hold a Level 5 Diploma in Business or a related discipline, or

  • Hold a Higher National Diploma (HND), Associate Degree, or an international equivalent representing at least two years of undergraduate study

  • Applicants with non-standard qualifications may be considered through Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) in accordance with ULDP policies

  • Degrees from unaccredited institutions or diploma mills will not be accepted

  • Admission decisions are made following an individual review of each applicantâ€™s academic and professional profile

  • TOEFL iBT 70 or equivalent.

What Makes This BEd Top-Up Different?

This programme focuses on progression, professional relevance, and efficiency.

  • Build directly on existing education qualifications
  • Study advanced, practice-oriented education content
  • Develop professional and transferable teaching skills
  • The programme bridges diploma-level study and bachelor-level outcomes with strong academic and professional alignment.

Education Areas and Professional Relevance

The programme addresses advanced undergraduate education themes, including:

  • Teaching and learning strategies
  • Curriculum design and instructional planning
  • Classroom management and learner support
  • Assessment and evaluation practices
  • Inclusive and learner-centred education
  • Applied educational research and final project development

These areas prepare graduates for professional teaching roles and further academic study.

Career and Academic Progression

Graduates commonly use the BEd Top-Up to:

  • Enter professional teaching or education support roles
  • Strengthen employability and teaching performance
  • Progress within schools, training centres, or educational organisations
  • Advance to postgraduate programmes such as a Master of Education
  • Enhance academic standing for international education opportunities
