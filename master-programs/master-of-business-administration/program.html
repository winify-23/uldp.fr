Master of Business Administration

Home > Master of Business Administration
Share :
Business Education for Real-World Leadership

Today’s organisations operate in an increasingly complex environment shaped by digital transformation, global competition, rapid innovation, and constant market change. Managers and leaders are expected not only to understand business theory, but to apply strategic thinking, lead teams effectively, and make sound decisions in real-world conditions.

The Master of Business Administration (MBA) is designed for professionals who want to strengthen their management capability, broaden their business perspective, and advance their careers in leadership, entrepreneurship, or specialised management roles.

This programme equips candidates with practical knowledge, strategic tools, and leadership skills that can be immediately applied across industries and organisational contexts.

Why This MBA Matters

Unlike purely academic business degrees, this MBA is practice-oriented and industry-relevant.

Participants engage with real business challenges, case studies, and applied projects that reflect contemporary organisational realities—such as strategy execution, leadership development, digital transformation, financial decision-making, and organisational performance.

The programme supports professionals who aim to:

  • Develop strong strategic and analytical thinking skills

  • Enhance leadership and people management capability

  • Improve decision-making in complex business environments

  • Accelerate career progression or transition into management roles

  • Build a solid foundation for entrepreneurship or consulting

Program Overview

The MBA programme focuses on core business and management competencies applicable across industries and sectors. The curriculum is structured into two integrated phases.

Phase 1 – Advanced Coursework and Professional Development

Participants undertake advanced study across six core modules in business and management, designed to:

  • Build a strong foundation in strategic management and leadership

  • Develop competence in finance, marketing, and organisational management

  • Strengthen analytical, problem-solving, and decision-making skills

  • Integrate contemporary business theory with professional practice

  • Enhance leadership effectiveness in diverse and international contexts

This phase ensures graduates develop well-rounded business knowledge and managerial capability essential for modern management roles.

Phase 2 – Applied Project or Strategic Integration

In the final phase, participants complete an applied project, capstone, or strategic analysis aligned with their professional interests, organisation, or industry.

  • Apply business concepts to real organisational challenges

  • Integrate learning across functional areas of management

  • Demonstrate practical problem-solving and strategic thinking

  • Deliver actionable recommendations with professional relevance

Learning Outcomes

Graduates of the Master of Business Administration will:

  • Demonstrate advanced knowledge of business and management principles

  • Apply strategic and analytical thinking to organisational challenges

  • Lead teams and projects effectively in dynamic environments

  • Make informed decisions using financial, operational, and market insights

  • Enhance professional credibility and career mobility across industries

Entry Requirements

Applicants to the Master of Business Administration must meet the following criteria:

  • Hold a Bachelor’s degree from a recognised institution or an equivalent qualification aligned with EQF Level 6

  • Applicants with non-standard qualifications may be considered through Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) in accordance with ULDP policies

  • Degrees from unaccredited institutions or diploma mills will not be accepted

  • Admission decisions are made following an individual review of each applicant’s academic and professional profile

English Language Requirements

Applicants whose previous education was not conducted in English are required to demonstrate English proficiency through one of the following:

  • CEFR Level B2 or equivalent

  • IELTS 6.5 (with Reading and Writing at 6.5 or above) or equivalent

  • TOEFL iBT 101 or equivalent

Tuitionfees

Tuition fees are subject to ULDP policies and may be revised without prior notice. Scholarships may be available for eligible applicants.

  • Application Fee: €400

  • Program Fee: €19,000

  • Payment Options: 2 Installments

The MBA is designed for professionals seeking to advance into management or leadership roles, including:

  • Early- to mid-career managers
  • Professionals transitioning into leadership positions
  • Entrepreneurs and business owners
  • Specialists seeking broader business knowledge
  • Professionals aiming for career acceleration or international mobility

This MBA emphasises practical learning and real-world application.

Participants:

  • Learn through case studies, applied projects, and real business scenarios
  • Develop skills directly relevant to workplace challenges
  • Gain a holistic understanding of how organisations operate and compete

The programme bridges business theory and professional practice, ensuring immediate workplace relevance.

The programme addresses key business functions and management themes, including:

  • Strategic management and leadership
  • Marketing and customer strategy
  • Financial and managerial decision-making
  • Human resource management
  • Digital transformation and innovation
  • Entrepreneurship and business development

These areas equip graduates to operate effectively across industries and organisational contexts.

Graduates of the MBA commonly use the qualification to:

  • Advance into supervisory, managerial, or leadership roles
  • Strengthen performance and credibility in current positions
  • Transition across industries or functional areas
  • Launch or scale entrepreneurial ventures
  • Prepare for further academic or professional development

The MBA enhances career flexibility, leadership readiness, and professional confidence.

Shaping Your Professional Journey

The MBA programme enables participants to design a learning pathway aligned with their career goals.

Through structured coursework and applied learning, participants develop the skills and mindset required to succeed in modern, competitive business environments.

Practical Business Skills

Develop immediately applicable skills in strategy, leadership, finance, and management.

Career-Focused Learning

Gain knowledge and tools that support promotion, career transition, and professional growth.

Global Business Perspective

Build the competence to operate effectively in international and multicultural business environments.
