Master of Arts in International Education and Pedagogy

Home > Master of Arts in International Education and Pedagogy
Share :
Advancing Education in a Global and Intercultural World

Education systems worldwide are evolving rapidly in response to globalisation, digital innovation, student mobility, and increasing cultural diversity. Educators are now expected not only to teach effectively, but to design inclusive learning environments, apply innovative pedagogical approaches, and respond to international educational standards and policies.

The Master of Arts in International Education and Pedagogy (MAIEP) is designed for educators and education professionals who seek advanced expertise in pedagogy, curriculum development, and international education practice.

This programme equips participants with advanced pedagogical knowledge, global perspectives, and research capability applicable across schools, higher education institutions, international organisations, and educational development contexts.

Why This MAIEP Matters

This programme addresses the real challenges faced by educators working in international, multicultural, and globally connected learning environments.

Participants develop the ability to:

  • Enhance teaching and learning effectiveness in international contexts

  • Design inclusive and globally informed curricula

  • Apply innovative pedagogical approaches and educational technologies

  • Advance careers in international education, academic coordination, or education leadership

  • Build a strong foundation for educational research or doctoral study

Program Overview

The Master of Arts in International Education and Pedagogy (MAIEP) focuses on advanced knowledge and professional competence in international education and pedagogy. The curriculum is structured into two integrated phases, combining coursework with independent research.

Phase 1 - Advanced Coursework and Professional Development

  • Strengthen understanding of contemporary pedagogical theories

  • Develop effective teaching strategies for diverse and multicultural learning environments

  • Enhance curriculum design and instructional planning skills

  • Integrate educational technologies into teaching and learning

  • Apply inclusive, culturally responsive, and learner-centred educational practices

This phase ensures graduates develop strong pedagogical competence and international educational awareness.

Phase 2 - Thesis or Research Project

  • Investigate real-world challenges in international education or pedagogy

  • Apply research methodologies to educational practice or policy analysis

  • Critically evaluate teaching and learning processes

  • Produce research-based recommendations with practical and academic relevance

Learning Outcomes

Upon completing the Master of Arts in International Education and Pedagogy, graduates will:

  • Demonstrate advanced knowledge of pedagogy and international education

  • Design effective and inclusive learning environments

  • Apply research-informed approaches to teaching and educational development

  • Integrate global perspectives into curriculum and instruction

  • Strengthen professional credibility in international and cross-cultural education settings

Entry Requirements

Applicants to the Master of Business Administration must meet the following criteria:

  • Hold a Bachelor’s degree from a recognized institution or an equivalent qualification aligned with EQF Level 6

  • Applicants with non-standard qualifications may be considered through Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) in accordance with ULDP policies

  • Degrees from unaccredited institutions or diploma mills will not be accepted

  • Admission decisions are made following an individual review of each applicant’s academic and professional profile

English Language Requirements

Applicants whose previous education was not conducted in English are required to demonstrate English proficiency through one of the following:

  • CEFR Level A2 or equivalent.

  • IELTS 6.5 (with Reading and Writing at 6.5 or above) or equivalent.

  • TOEFL iBT 101 or equivalent.

Tuitionfees

Tuitionfees are subject to ULDP policies and may be revised without prior notice. Scholarships may be available for eligible applicants.

  • Application Fee: €400

  • Program Fee: €19,000

  • Payment Options: 2 Installments

This programme is designed for:

  • Teachers and educators working in international or multicultural settings
  • Academic coordinators and education managers
  • Professionals in international schools, NGOs, or educational organisations
  • Education specialists seeking global pedagogical expertise
  • Graduates planning careers in international education

This programme stands out for its emphasis on global pedagogy and applied educational practice.

Participants will have the opportunity to:

  • Connect pedagogical theory with international education contexts
  • Develop strategies for diverse and inclusive learning environments
  • Engage in reflective and research-informed professional development

The programme bridges educational theory, international practice, and research.

Graduates are prepared to work across a range of contexts, including:

  • International and bilingual schools
  • Higher education institutions
  • Educational NGOs and development organisations
  • Cross-border and mobility-focused education programmes

Career and Professional Impact

Graduates commonly use the qualification to:

  • Advance careers in international teaching and education leadership
  • Move into curriculum development or academic coordination roles
  • Contribute to educational policy or programme development
  • Strengthen professional standing in global education environments
  • Prepare for doctoral study in education or pedagogy

Shaping Your International Education Journey

The MAIEP enables participants to design a learning pathway aligned with their professional goals in international education. Through advanced coursework and independent research, participants develop the expertise, perspective, and confidence required to succeed in globally connected educational environments.

Global Pedagogical Expertise

Develop teaching and learning strategies suited to international and multicultural contexts.

Research-Informed Educational Practice

Strengthen the ability to analyse, evaluate, and improve educational systems and practices.

International Career Pathways

Build credentials and competence for careers across borders, institutions, and cultures.
