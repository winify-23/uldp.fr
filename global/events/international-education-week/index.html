International Education Week

IEW 2025  |  November 17 - 21, 2025 

International Education Week (IEW) is a joint initiative of the U.S. Department of State and the U.S. Department of Education.

The purpose of IEW is to promote the benefits of international education and cultural exchange. IEW is celebrated across the United States on an annual basis during the third week of November.

At the University of Houston, IEW is an opportunity to showcase the various global programs and international opportunities as well as celebrate our cultural diversity

The centerpiece of IEW is GlobalFest!, a global engagement resource fair, to be held on Monday November 17 from 11:30 to 1:00pm in the Student Center Plaza. The rain location is the Houston Room.

  • This main event includes cultural performances, international snacks, tabling by campus groups with a global focus, and the chance to win gift cards playing Passport Bingo!

Throughout the week, you can join the global fun and win prizes!

  • Learning Abroad Instagram photo contest: Submit your global photo in one of these categories: Culture and Society, Landscape/Cityscape and Cougar Pride. Top three winners win an Amazon gift card and social media exposure for their photo.

IEW activities are planned annually by the IEW Co-chairs and Committee. The 2025 IEW Co-chairs are:

  • Marisol Gonzalez, Institute for Global Engagement
  • Stacey Michael, Institute for Global Engagement
  • Melissa Melendez, Dean of Students Office
  • Ashley Simmons Coffey, International Student and Scholar Services

The IEW Committee will take shape shortly after classes begin on August 25.
