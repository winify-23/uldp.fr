ELEVATE YOUR CAREER & YOUR IMPACT

Grow professionally and personally with a graduate degree, certification or certificate from the University of Houston College of Education. Our alumni are leading the way in education, health and wellness, research and policy, and beyond. We invite you to join our community of learners and leaders at one of the nation’s most diverse public research universities. Become a Coog today!

How to Apply

Apply Now

  • Meaningful Coursework

    Our courses connect directly to real-world issues, mixing the latest research with practical experience. You’ll feel challenged and inspired as you use your learning to make a positive impact in the community.

  • Supportive Professors

    Our faculty members are leaders in their fields, and they’re dedicated to helping you achieve your academic and professional goals. You’ll meet mentors, research collaborators and lifelong connections.

  • Successful Students

    Our students graduate well prepared to thrive in rewarding careers in the Houston region and around the globe. The Cougar alumni network extends far to keep you forever linked to ongoing support and new opportunities.

PROGRAMS TO MEET YOUR GOALS

Level up your skills with an M.Ed., Ed.D., Ph.D., certification or certificate. And stand out in the job market with credentials from a Tier One research university! Many of our graduate programs are designed for working professionals, and we have a mix of online, face-to-face and hybrid offerings for your convenience. Ready to find your place to belong? Explore our degrees and programs.

AWARD-WINNING ONLINE Education PROGRAMS

Our online M.Ed. programs are nationally recognized for excellence, ranking among the top 20 on U.S. News & World Report’s 2025 list. These online master’s degree offerings include tracks in curriculum and instruction, higher education, and special populations.

Discover the Heart of Houston

As a UH student, you’ll get to study and live in one of the most culturally diverse cities in America. Enjoy amazing museums, global cuisine, arts performances, professional sports and more. Houston boasts the world’s largest medical center and is a hub for innovation in technology and energy. Are you ready to make Houston home?
